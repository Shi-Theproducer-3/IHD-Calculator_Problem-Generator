Calculator rounds to 4 decimal places, answer for the equation rounds to 3.

Generate a random math equation by clicking Generate. Solve with the help of the calculator if needed! After you solve it check your answer with the Check Answer button. If you get stuck or you want to check the answer at anytime, double tap the Reveal button.

*Hint*...if there isn't addition or subtraction on either side of the = sign, it's probably zero...*Blob*
